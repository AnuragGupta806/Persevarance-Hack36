About Us

Since Covid pandemic came into effect, many aspects of our life came to a standstill including our learning. This website is a common place for students and teachers to connect virtually and continue to interact with each other.

  • Lots of features to help with easy interaction.
  • Live Chatting feature to clear doubts and discuss matters
  • Unique profile of each indivisual for managing one's resources

In recent years, many such apps were developed to help people tackle the problem of virtual education through MOOC and other platforms. My website is just a small part of such a concept to show that it is not difficult to do such things by yourself.
